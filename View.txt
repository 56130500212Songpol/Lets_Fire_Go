**1.View**

1.1) Show number that represent a characteristic of a corresponding location
- 0 to indicate an empty cell with no tree or a burnt tree
- 1 to indicate a cell with a non-burning tree
- 2 to indicate a cell with a burning tree.


1.2) Show color
- Green is indicating a non-burning tree
- Red is a burning tree
- Yellow is an empty cell.
